Set background image

For your responsive product templates, you can upload a background image that will show as a banner image for the tickets and memberships delivered as a web page.

On the Product template page, in the section 'Responsive' click Upload background image.

Once uploaded, you will be able to view the uploaded image, as well as delete it.

Note

The possibility to use a Responsive template is available to specific Venues only. You can request Vintia support to enable this feature for your Venue.
