Admin

Enviso Admin allows you to manage users and user roles for Enviso Sales, Enviso Trade, Enviso Booking, Enviso Forms and eLoxx - based on your license for an Enviso app.

Depending on whether you are a venue or a reseller user you can carry out certain functionalities from the Admin app.

Some of the activities that are possible on Enviso Admin are configuring languages for data translation, managing access control and configure visitor settings, sales points and export transactions.  

You can consult the online and onsite sales points that have been integrated from Recreatex with Enviso. However, if sales points are not synchronized from Recreatex, you can create sales points in Enviso Admin.
